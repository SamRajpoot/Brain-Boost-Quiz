Privacy Policy - Brain Boost Quiz

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Brain Boost Quiz is an offline quiz app.

Data Collection

  • The app does not require account creation or login.
  • The app does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.
  • Quiz content is bundled locally in the app package.

Local Device Storage

  • The app stores high scores and simple settings (dark mode and sound toggle) locally on your device using local preferences.
  • This data never leaves the device.

Third-Party Services

  • No analytics SDK is included.
  • No ad SDK is included.
